About Us


Welcome to Beanzu, where delightful moments are brewed alongside your favorite beverages! I'm Krizia, the passionate owner of this unique pet cafe nestled in the heart of joy and furry companionship. At Beanzu, we bring the love of Shih Tzus and the joy of coffee together, creating a haven for pet enthusiasts and caffeine aficionados alike. Immerse yourself in the cozy ambiance, savor delicious treats, and enjoy the company of our adorable Shih Tzu residents. Beanzu is not just a cafe; it's a vibrant community where wagging tails and warm smiles come together to create unforgettable experiences. Join us for a paw-some time at Beanzu, where every sip is accompanied by the purest form of happiness!
