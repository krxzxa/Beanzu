About Beanzu

Greetings from Beanzu: An Adorable Coffee Experience Beanzu is not your typical coffee shop; rather, it's a sanctuary where the lovely presence of our cherished Shih Tzus blends with the scent of freshly ground coffee. Tucked away in the core of our neighborhood, Beanzu is a welcoming haven where dog friendship reigns supreme, offering more than simply a spot to get your favorite brew.

The Beanzu Distinction: Version Shih Tzu Beanzu celebrates the special relationship that exists between lovers of Shih Tzus and coffee. Imagine yourself, your favorite coffee, and a room full with Shih Tzus' endearing presence. Our area has been carefully planned to provide a peaceful setting where Shih Tzus and their human companions may relax, interact, and build enduring memories.

Coffee companionship with dogs: Imagine having a crisp croissant or a velvety coffee with your Shih Tzu curled up next to you. At Beanzu, we elevate the coffee experience beyond recognition by bringing our animal friends' happiness and company into the mix. Every visit is an occasion to enjoy life's little joys together with your Shih Tzu.

Paw-some Treats: Not only is our food suitable for customers with two legs. Beanzu is pleased to present a delicious assortment of sweets made especially for our dog friends. Everything from handmade dog biscuits to puppuccinos is tailored to please the pickiest palates of Shih Tzus and make sure they enjoy their time with their human companions.

Warm Spots and Waggly Tails: Beanzu is a community center that welcomes Shih Tzus of all sizes, not just a coffee shop. We have spaces for play for the more active dogs, quiet spots for private times, and many of places for your Shih Tzu to meet new people. Every visit is certain to be an absolute blast.

Brewed with Love: We are as dedicated to quality with our coffee as we are with our canine visitors. Every cup of coffee at Beanzu is lovingly made, and each Shih Tzu is given the consideration and care they merit.

Step inside Beanzu, where coffee meets canine friendship, and let the warmth of our environment embrace you and your Shih Tzu. We can't wait to share the delight of a Beanzu encounter with you and your canine companion!
